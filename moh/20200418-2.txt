942 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 18 Apr 2020

  As of 18 April 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 942 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories.

2.  The number of local cases has continued to decline, with
14 Singaporeans/ Permanent Residents among the cases today.

3.  We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.



MINISTRY OF HEALTH
18 APRIL 2020
