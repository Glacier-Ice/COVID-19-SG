931 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 26 Apr 2020

                 As of 26 April 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH)
has preliminarily confirmed an additional 931 cases of COVID-19
infection in Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit
holders residing in foreign worker dormitories. 15 cases are
Singaporeans/ Permanent Residents.
2.            We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.
