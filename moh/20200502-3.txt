447 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 02 May 2020

1. As of 2 May 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 447 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. Four cases are Singaporeans/ Permanent
Residents.
2. We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
2 MAY 2020
