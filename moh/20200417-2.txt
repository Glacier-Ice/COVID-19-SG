623 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 17 Apr 2020

         As of 17 April 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 623 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the majority of whom are foreign workers in the dormitories.

2.     We are still working through the details of these cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued later tonight.
