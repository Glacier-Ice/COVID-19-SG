PASSING OF PATIENT WITH COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 03 Apr 2020

             The Ministry of Health has confirmed that a patient has
passed away from complications due to COVID-19 infection on 3 April 2020
at 1.55am. She was an 86 year-old female Singapore Citizen who had no
recent travel history to affected countries and regions.

2.           She was admitted to the National Centre for Infectious
Diseases (NCID) on 31 March, and was confirmed to have COVID-19
infection on the same day. She eventually succumbed to the infection.

3.           NCID has reached out to her family and is extending
assistance to them.
