573 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 04 May 2020

                As of 4 May 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 573 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. Five cases are Singaporeans/ Permanent
Residents.
2.             We are still working through the details of the cases,
and further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will
be issued tonight.
