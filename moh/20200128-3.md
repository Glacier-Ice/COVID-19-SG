    <p>Published Date: 28 Jan 2020</p> <p><strong><u>Update on Wuhan Coronavirus Infection in Singapore</u></strong></p><p><u>Update on suspect cases</u><br></p><p>As of 28 January, 12pm, 91 of the suspect cases have tested negative for the Wuhan coronavirus, and seven have tested positive. Test results for the remaining 72 cases are pending.<br></p><p><u>Update on contact tracing for confirmed cases</u><br></p><p>MOH has identified 160 close contacts from seven confirmed cases. Of the 130 who are still in Singapore, 116 have been contacted and are being quarantined or isolated. Efforts are ongoing to contact the remaining 14 close contacts.<br></p></body>
