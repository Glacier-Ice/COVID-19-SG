528 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION (30 APR)


Published Date: 30 Apr 2020

1.     As of 30 April 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 528 cases of COVID-19 infection in
Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing in
foreign worker dormitories. Six cases are Singaporeans/ Permanent
Residents.

2.     We are still working through the details of the cases, and
further updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be
issued tonight.

MINISTRY OF HEALTH

30 APRIL 2020
