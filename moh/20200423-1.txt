1,037 NEW CASES OF COVID-19 INFECTION


Published Date: 23 Apr 2020

1. As of 23 April 2020, 12pm, the Ministry of Health (MOH) has
preliminarily confirmed an additional 1,037 cases of COVID-19 infection
in Singapore, the vast majority of whom are Work Permit holders residing
in foreign worker dormitories. 21 cases are Singaporeans/ Permanent
Residents.
2. We are still working through the details of the cases, and further
updates will be shared via the MOH press release that will be issued
tonight.
MINISTRY OF HEALTH
23 APRIL 2020
