                                                      DIRECTOR GENERAL HEALTH
                           
                                                                       CURRENT SITUATION OF INFECTION
                                       Coronavirus DISEASE 2019 (COVID-19) IN MALAYSIA
                           
                          As of February 25, 2020, the cumulative number of positive cases of disease
                          Coronavirus 2019 (COVID-19) were reported in Malaysia remains
                          22 cases. Of these, 20 have been recovered
                          completely and discharged from the ward. And two (2) people,
                          Case 16 and Case 22, was treated in hospital and they are in
                          stable condition. Total Patient-under-investigation (PUI) reported
                          to Crisis Preparedness and Response Center (CPRC), Ministry
                          Health (MOH) until February 25, 2020 was
                          796 cases.
                           
                          MOH always ensure preparedness and response to pandemic
                          or the occurrence of infectious diseases can be effectively implemented
                          and good at all levels, including at the International Port of Entry
                          (PMA) State. As of February 23, 2020, a total of 7,617,964 have been
