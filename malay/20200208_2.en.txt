       These groups include children, senior citizens aged 65 years and over,

       low and pregnant women. If these people still need to be present to the hospital,
       they are advised to practice self-care such as
       following:

           Avoid touching your eyes, nose and mouth without washing
            first hand;

           Wash your hands often with soap and water for at
            least for a period of 20 seconds or use hand sanitizers;

           Avoid approaching those who have symptoms of infection
            respiratory tract such as cough or colds;

           To practice cough etiquette (cough etiquette) - that is shut up and
            nose with a tissue when coughing or sneezing, the next
            wash hands immediately;

           If you have symptoms of respiratory tract infection, please wear cover
            nose and mouth and keep a safe distance (ie at
            least 1 meter) from others in the vicinity.
