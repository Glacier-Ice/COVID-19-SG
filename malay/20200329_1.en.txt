       CAPACITY TO CARRY OUT LABORATORY TESTS
       DETECTION COVID-19

       Initially, the Medical Research Institute (Institute for
       Medical Research; IMR) is the only laboratory
       directly involved in carrying out the detection test
       COVID-19 in Malaysia. Laboratory capacity is now
       expanded and strengthened, involving 25 laboratories in
       the whole country. The lab network consists of:

         • Eighteen (18) MOH laboratories - the IMR, Laboratory
           National Public Health (NPHL) Sungai Buloh, two
           twelve (12) laboratories in the MOH hospitals and four


          (4) Public Health Laboratory at the state level (ie
          Johor, Kelantan, Perak and Sabah); and

         • Seven (7) private laboratories - the Healthcare Technical Services (M) Sdn. Bhd.,
          Pantai Premier Pathology Sdn. Bhd., Neogenix Laboratories
          PT. Bhd., Clinipath (M) Sdn. Bhd., BP Clinical Lab Sdn. Bhd.
          (Branch Glenmarie), Sunway Medical Center and Gribbles
          Pathology Sdn. Bhd.

       Lastly, to help the Ministry of Health to increase the capacity test
       COVID-19 detection; Ministry of Higher Education and
       Ministry of Science, Technology and Innovation (MOSTI)
       provides ten (10) additional diagnostic laboratory at the institution
       higher education, university teaching hospitals (HPU), including a
       (1) mobile laboratory that can be sent to hotspots.
       All ten (10) additional diagnostic laboratories will be able to
       perform detection testing 42.420 a month or 1,414
       day.

       above, while 67.6 percent have chronic diseases such as;
