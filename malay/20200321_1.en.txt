       RELATED HEALTH ADVICE COVID-19

       Motion Control Order was implemented from March 18
       until March 31, 2020, which among others include bans
       comprehensive movement to curb the spread of the disease
       COVID-19 infections in Malaysia. During the enforcement period
       This order, people were asked to stay at home.

       While living in the house, Malaysians advised
       to stay active, whether through exercise or physical activity.
       Nowadays there are a variety of home exercise can
       do, for example:

           Form of cardio exercise for those who have
       •
           exercise bike or treadmill at home.

           Exercises that use your own body weight, such as
       •
           push-ups, squats, planks, burpees and lounges (which
           using a variety of muscle without equipment
           special), a Tabata and light exercise like yoga
           or tai chi.

       Malaysians can also stay active by doing various
       dance forms and take the opportunity to do spring
       cleaning in the house. Studies show that exercise
       and physical activity is important for maintaining health
       mental to improve mood and relieve anxiety
       and anxiety. It helps sharpen the mind, increases
       muscle strength and aerobic power.

       If unhealthy, virtual contact and follow the health advisory
