      CURRENT STATUS OF ACTIVITIES CENTERED APPROACH
      (TARGETED APPROACH)
      As informed earlier, the Ministry of Health has intensified
      activity detection, screening, testing, isolation and treatment
      especially to high-risk target groups with
      focused approach (targeted approach). This was carried out
      in collaboration with various government agencies and non
      government.
      Among the activities carried out focused approach to
      stopping the spread of COVID-19 is like
      Bolted Movement Control Order (PKPD) in the area-
      high-risk areas, detection activities at school or
      religious schools, and in particular quarantine station for the People
      Malaysians returning from abroad. So far the results of
      conducted activities focused approach, the MOH has been successful
      detect more positive cases COVID-19.
      Up to 26 April 2020 at 12 noon, for activities PKPD
      found a total of 21,466 people have been tested COVID-19 in
      in PKPD area. So far, 638 cases tested positive for COVID-
      19 (2.97 per cent). It was found that the number of positive COVID-
      19, a total of 388 cases (61 per cent) were non-citizens
      Malaysia and the remaining 250 cases (39 per cent) were nationals
      Malaysia.
