      CURRENT STATUS OF LABORATORY CAPACITY TO PERFORM
      DETECTION TEST AND VERIFICATION COVID-19
      Initially, the Medical Research Institute (Institute for
      Medical Research; IMR) is the only laboratory
      directly involved in carrying out the detection test
      COVID-19 in Malaysia. Laboratory capacity is now
      expanded and strengthened, involving 43 laboratories
      from the public sector, universities and the private sector.


       Currently, the maximum capacity of the laboratory to carry out tests
       detection and confirmation COVID-19 was 11,546.
       RELATED HEALTH ADVICE COVID-19

       Ministry of Health called on Malaysians to comply with all instructions
       associated with the control of movement. failure
       comply with any instructions, if convicted
       the offense is punishable under Section 24
       Act 342.
       While at home, the Ministry of Health would like to advise the public
       in order to always maintain a high level of cleanliness on
       each time, such as frequent hand washing, using
       water and soap and always practice social distance (social

       distancing) of at least 1 meter away from other people.
       Ministry of Health will continue to monitor the development of events COVID-19
       through all the information available and will be informed
       the people from time to time.
