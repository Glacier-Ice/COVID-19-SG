         COVID-19 INFECTION RISK AMONG THE ELDERLY
         PATIENTS AND CHRONIC DISEASE
         As was informed earlier, the senior
         Gold found quite affected by the epidemic COVID-19 where
         There are many cases of infection and 19 deaths in COVID
         among the age group 60 years and above. Senior citizens are
         a group at high risk for complications and death
         COVID-19 infection due to age and illness
         chronic. This is a global issue facing the country-
         countries affected by the epidemic.
         Statistics show there are two (2) age groups in society
         The highest positive cases compared COVID-19 age group
         the other, that is 20 years and 24 years and 56 years
         up to 59 years. This can be seen in the chart number of positive cases
         COVID-19 by age. But when the statistics the number of cases
         COVID-19 is matched with the population of each group
         age, can clearly be seen that the incidence rate for the 19th COVID
         100,000 population in Malaysia is the highest for the group
         age 55 to 59 years and 60 to 64 years.

       HEALTH ADVICE TO THE ELDERLY AND PATIENTS
       CHRONIC DISEASE

       Available kebolehpulihan infection and the prognosis for recovery
       COVID-19 is better if treatment is started early,
       thus very important for the elderly to society
       Although the treatment has only SOON
       mild symptoms such as fever, cough, runny nose or sore throat.
       It is the responsibility of family members such as children or
       guardian who lives with the elderly in order to ensure
       senior citizens:
       • get immediate medical attention if unwell
       • Supply of adequate medicines and treatment appointments follow
       • Goods sufficient daily necessities;
       • The adoption of a high level of personal hygiene such as frequent
         Washing hands with water and soap;
       • Adopt a safe social distance (social distancing)
         at least 1 meter.
