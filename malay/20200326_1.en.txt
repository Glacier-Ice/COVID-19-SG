       MEMBERS POTENTIAL HEALTH AND INFECTION COVID-19

       MOH would like to inform that as of March 26, 2020
       at 12:00 pm, it was reported that there are 80 members
       MOH health tested positive for the disease COVID-19.

       So far, NO cases MOH health personnel
       due to the operation of the 19 positive cases at the facility COVID
       Health MOH. Of these, a total of 39 members
       is a cluster of events in Hospital Teluk Intan, Perak.
       Taking into account the current situation, the Ministry of Health has taken
       all health personnel and support personnel Hospital
       Teluk Intan to test detection COVID-19 in
       hospital.

       All members and front-line health (liner)
       advised to adopt preventive measures
       as has been suggested, especially in ensuring
       hands are always clean, adopting a social (social
       distancing) and the use of personal protective equipment (PPE) by
       requirements.


       Medical practitioners in hospitals and private clinics also is
       forefront in the provision of health services to
       community. Ministry of Health would like to advise all practitioners
       private sector to adopt preventive measures such as
       described above mainly adopts the use of
       mask (face mask) when handling
       patient and use appropriate PPE when performing
       any clinical procedures.
       HEALTH SERVICES FOR PREGNANT WOMEN AND CHILDREN
       HEALTH CLINIC, MOTHER AND CHILD HEALTH CLINICS
       CLINICS AND VILLAGES

       Despite the epidemic situation COVID 19th, MOH wants
       stresses that the Health Services Maternal and Child
       is running normally. Health care personnel will
       carry out home visits to service last mom
       to ensure delivery of maternal and child health .For
       pregnant women and parents who are outside, may be
       services in government health clinics or
       private close.

       Therefore, pregnant women are required to undergo inspection by
       given an appointment date. Immunization services to
       infants and children also continue as usual. Parents
       are required to comply with the immunization schedule and the date of appointment
       has been set. Ministry of Health would like to remind of the importance of
       adhere to the schedule for childhood immunizations prevent
       outbreak of disease preventative vaccine (vaccine preventable
       diseases).

       Nearby health.
