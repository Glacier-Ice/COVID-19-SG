       PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT (PERSONAL PROTECTIVE
       EQUIPMENT - PPE) FOR HEALTH OFFICER
       Ministry of Health would like to inform about the readiness and status
       Latest Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) for use
       Health officials in handling cases COVID-19.
       As is well known, the issue is the issue of lack of PPE
       global where almost all countries affected by the contagion
       COVID epidemic-19. The Ministry of Health facilities, too, found the use of PPE
       has increased dramatically following the pandemic COVID-19
       this.
       Personal protective equipment or Personal Protective Equipment
       (PPE) is a key requirement for officers
       Health forefront of the pandemic COVID-19.
       There are several types of PPE used by officers
       health, for example:
         • Footwear / boot cover - the remaining stock of 78 days
         • Protective head cover - the remaining stock of 52 days;
         • The types of masks 3-ply mask - stock remaining 47
          day;
         • Tool masks N-95 surgical type mask - tray
          stock 37 days;
         • Disposable Face Shield (eye visor, goggles or face shield) -
          25 days remaining stock


       • Fluid Resistant Disposable Apron (Long Sleeves With Cuff) -
        the remaining stock of 23 days;
       • Jumpsuit / Protective Coverall - 19 days remaining stock
       • Dress common plastics, and other types of PPE.
     To ensure continuous supplies of PPE is, MOH and
     is constantly running the procurement process for PPE
     bulk purchases at the federal level and the state level. MOH
     working closely with the National Security Council, the Agency
     National Disaster Management (DMA) and other central agencies
     to ensure that issues relating to this stock could be considered
     special. PPE contribution by various private parties, corporate
     Non-governmental organizations (NGOs) and donors continue to welcome and
     millions of thanks to them.
     To closely monitor stock levels and the utilization rate for
     each type of PPE in every country, the Ministry of Health has been using the system
     reporting and data bases on simple lines, in which
     it's run by the Officer in each Department of Health
     State (SHD), hospitals and District Health Office
     (PKD) (including all clinic under each
     PKD). This report is closely monitored and action mobilization stocks will
     made.
     List names, phone numbers and e-mail for each employee
     the state has been notified to assist the public and
     staff to respond in the event of
     lack of PPE. Revisions will prompt mobilization and action
     carried out according to the needs and priorities.
     It should be noted also that each type of PPE should be worn
     according to which the health officer on duty, namely
     as set out in the Operational Guidelines
     COVID-19 in Malaysia No.5 / 2020 (Annex 8: The Infection
     Prevention And Control (IPC) Measures). Not all officers
     health should wear to all types of PPE at the time they
     duty. This is vital to ensure the rate and pattern
     the use of PPE in MOH health facilities. Accordingly, the


        Ministry of Health recommends a careful and prudent use of PPE by
        all health workers as it is used for
        ongoing.

        RELATED HEALTH ADVICE COVID-19

        Health authorities urge people to perform
        with their respective responsibilities:
           1. Adopt an optimal level of cleanliness at all
             time, such as frequent hand washing using water
             and soap
           2. Adopt a safe social (social distancing)
             at least 1 meter away from other people.
        Ministry of Health will continue to monitor the development of events COVID-19
        through all the information available and will be informed
        the people from time to time.
