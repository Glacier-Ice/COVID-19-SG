     HEALTH ADVICE

     Subsequently, wishes to advise Malaysians to continue
     obedience to the Order of Movement Control (CSC). Though
     developments in this case is absolutely positive reduction
     but people still need to be at home,
     practice safe social distance (social distancing)
     at least 1 meter away from other people as well
     practice good personal hygiene such as frequent high
     wash hands with soap and water.
     This approach is important in breaking the chain of transmission
     infection.

     MOH will continue to monitor the progress of events
     COVID-19 through the information obtained and will
     known to people from time to time.
