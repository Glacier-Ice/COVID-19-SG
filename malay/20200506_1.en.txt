       LATEST INFORMATION AND QUARANTINE FACILITY IN CENTER
       TREATMENT OF LOW-RISK COVID 19th, GARDEN EXPO
       AGRICULTURE Serdang (MAEPS)
       Ministry of Health would like to inform the current status and Quarantine Center
       Treatment of Low-Risk COVID 19th Agricultural Exposition Park
       Malaysia Serdang (MAEPS). The center was developed as
       one of preparation and readiness for action
       enhance the capacity of MOH facilities to accommodate patients
       COVID-19.
       As informed earlier, this center has been operational
       since 21 April 2020 and has received positive patients COVID-19
       low-risk or no symptoms.
       Services provided are:
         • clinical services such as in-patient ward for patients
          COVID-19
         • room resuscitation to stabilize the patient in a state
          emergency (if required),
         • ambulance services,


       • pharmacy services,
       • x-ray services,
       • pathology services.
     There are two (2) board with a total capacity of 604
     beds. The center is also equipped with a lounge, a

     (WiFi) for patient comfort. For officers of health,
     The center has a dedicated work area and a lounge.
     The center is equipped with a closed circuit camera control and under
     the police security around the clock.

     Health (ILKKM) Sungai Buloh and some officials

       RELATED HEALTH ADVICE COVID-19

       Following the aggressive acts of the Government using the
       focused approach (targeted approach), more cases have been
       can be identified, and now the number of cases found to be slightly lower.
       But this situation is changing by the day and it was too
       early to declare the danger COVID-19 as low.

       Ministry of Health would like to remind that the decline in the number of cases not
       It should make people complacent and too take


      Easy Motion Control Order Conditional (PKPB). instead
      people are urged to continue to maintain all efforts for
      This in fighting the virus with the Government. Thus, the Ministry of Health
      seek the cooperation of the Malaysian people to each other
      maintain and protect themselves from the virus COVID-19. This can be achieved
      by:

        1. Comply PKPB;
        2. Adopt an optimal level of cleanliness at all
          time, such as frequent hand washing using water
          and soap;
        3. Adopt a safe social (social distancing)
          at least 1 meter from other people;
        4. Adopt new norms in life
          everyday.

      Ministry of Health will continue to monitor the incidence of this infection
      through all the information available and will be informed
      the people from time to time.
