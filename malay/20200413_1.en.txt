       RELATED HEALTH ADVICE COVID-19

       Ministry of Health wishes to advise Malaysians to play a role
       each in addressing this pandemic. For health and
       the welfare of all, it is very important that the public
       obedience to the Order of Movement Control (CSC) in Malaysia.

       Malaysians are advised to stay at home and practice
       a secure social (social distancing) at least 1
       meters from others. Continue high hygiene practices
       for example washing hands frequently with water and
       soap, practice a healthy lifestyle and a balanced diet.

       Ministry of Health will continue to monitor the development of events COVID-19
       through all the information available and will be informed
       the people from time to time.
