        HOME STUDENT MOVEMENT INSTITUTE OF EDUCATION
        HIGH (IPT)
        As announced by the Minister of Higher Education, student-
        Students in Higher Education Institutions of Public and Private Polytechnics
        and community colleges be allowed to start a movement for
        gradually to return to their homeland
        starting 27 April 2020.
        The collaboration and discussion between the Ministry of
        Education (MOE) and the Ministry of Health, the guidelines have been
        developed where it covers the health aspects of
        students who want to return home. center
        Health University (PKU), each of which will make screening
        for students (body temperature, filters symptoms of cough, colds,
        sore throat or difficulty breathing) before being allowed to proceed
        trip.

        Along the way back to my hometown, students
        advised to follow the steps
        prevention as follows, namely:
           • practice safe social distance (social distancing)
             at least 1 meter from other people;
           • hygiene, frequent hand washing
             with soap and water;


          • encourage the use of face masks
             (Facemask) along the way.

        HEALTH ADVICE COVID-19
        Ministry of Health urged people to remain obedient to the command
        Control Movement (BCM). For the head of the household who have to go out
        purchase of essential items of daily life,
        must always take preventive measures
        such as:
          • Do not leave the home if unwell or
             have any symptoms;
          • Avoid out in pairs or in groups;
          • Use hand sanitizer / hand washing with water and
             soap before and after purchase;
          • Practicing safe social distance (social distancing)
             at least 1 meter from other people;
          • Avoid 3C: a crowded area (Crowded place), the
             Cconfined narrow space), speaking to the distance
             close (Close conversation);
          • Limit your time in public places;
          • Should have a mask
             (Facemask) may be used, especially if the
             common area that many people;
        The following steps can help decide chain
        COVID-19 transmission.
        Ministry of Health will continue to monitor the development of events COVID-19
        through all the information available and will be informed
        the people from time to time.
