       RELATED HEALTH ADVICE COVID-19
        
       Motion Control Order was implemented from March 18
       until March 31, 2020, which among others include bans
       comprehensive movement to curb the spread of the disease
       COVID-19 infections in Malaysia. During this period, people
       asked to stay at home. Thus, the Ministry of Health would like to advise
       the public on the following items to ensure
       optimal health is obtained:
        
         1. Adopt a healthy lifestyle. This includes always
           adopt healthy diets, physical inactivity,
           No smoking in the house and always calm and
           practice positive stress management.
            
         2. Ensure the food you buy is in good condition
           and do not go beyond the expiry date. The food was bad
           can cause food poisoning.
        
         3. For the public who have a chronic illness, be sure
           adequate supply of medicines and continue to take medication
           according to the schedule.
        
         4. Perform an environment in homes and premises
           each including disinfection activities.
        
       If unhealthy, virtual contact and follow the health advisory
       The sessions will be held every day from 9.00
          Webinar
       morning through the following link:
        
              http://www.doctoroncall.com.my/coronavirus
        
       The public can also call the Crisis Preparedness
       and Response Center (CPRC), National Ministry of Health. More information
                                                          
        
       about the incident COVID-19 in Malaysia can also be found
       through a telegraph channel CPRC Ministry of Health;
       https://t.me/cprckkm.

       through all the information available and will be informed
