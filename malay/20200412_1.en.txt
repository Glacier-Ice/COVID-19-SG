         Ministry of Health Malaysia has always given priority
         to the safety of health personnel, particularly
         front-liners (liner) who worked tirelessly
         in the face of a pandemic COVID-19 in the interest of
         Country.

         Therefore, various measures have been taken for the purpose of keeping
         safety and health personnel, including:


        • assessment of needs and stocks of personal protective equipment at the facilities for
            always ensure sufficient supply of PPE;
        • all cases of SARI and lung infection (pneumonia)
            as COVID-19 infection until proven otherwise;
        • strengthen the evaluation and triaging isolation
            patients with suspected symptoms when COVID-19
            come to health facilities as well as giving them the tools
            Cover your nose and mouth (facemask) to be used;
        • ensuring that the members of the patient care tested positive
            COVID-19 should use personal protective equipment (PPE)
            as suggested, and members who treat patients
            not suspected COVID-19 using the nose cap
            and mouth (facemask);
        • ensuring that members adopt all measures
            prevention such as hand hygiene and practice of distance
            secure social (social distancing) of at least 1 meter
            as and when appropriate;
        Ministry of Health would like to advise all members of the health and the line
