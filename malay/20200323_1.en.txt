                          PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT
           SUPPLY (PPE) FOR
           USE OF HEALTH

           MOH would like to inform that the issue of shortage
           PPE is now a global issue. MOH has always
           priority to security personnel. Therefore,
           The Ministry of Health has taken various measures to ensure
           an adequate supply of PPE for each health facility through
           supply coordinator with the state health department.

           Up to now, the MOH has secured the supply of stock
           PPE of 33 million units of capacity utilization in
           all health facilities and will be distributed this week. apart
           Moreover, the government has also received a donation
           PPE supplies from various parties and a total of 3 million units of PPE
           were distributed to include all healthcare facilities according to the needs.
           MOH would like to express our sincere appreciation to
           all parties for their contributions.

           RELATED HEALTH ADVICE COVID-19

           Ministry of Health would like to advise people to stay at home
           during the period of enforcement of the control of movement.
           People should practice a healthy lifestyle and
           adopt an optimal level of personal hygiene. Many people
           also advised to carry out cleanup activities
           residential environment and their premises, including
           disinfection activities.

           If unhealthy, virtual contact and follow the health advisory session
           The webinar will be held each day starts at 9.00 am
           through the following links for consultation:

                        http://www.doctoroncall.com.my/coronavirus
