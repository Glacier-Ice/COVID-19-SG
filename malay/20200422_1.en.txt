          HEALTH ADVICE IN CONJUNCTION WITH RELATED COVID-19
          RAMADAN

          As all know, the spread of the pandemic COVID 19th
          now sweeping the globe has affected a very
          significantly to the practice and norms of everyday life. this
          including changes to the way the religious practices and
          cultural tradition of visiting such as culture,
          Religious gatherings and receptions that are
          Among the unique identity of a pluralistic society in the country.

          The tradition and practice of worship in Ramadan for Muslims
          around the world will also be affected as much
          country is implementing motion control and border
          to curb the spread of infection COVID-19 in each country
          respectively.

          Taking a cue from the spread of infection COVID-19 in
          Pengerang clusters and sub-clusters Rembau People
          to remain obedient to the Movement Control Order (PKP) and
          avoid doing any activity calling the visit to
          home friends and relatives. This included a trip across
          states, districts and sub-districts or attending rallies large-
          scale (including religious ceremonies and receptions). MOH
          concerned that such activity has indeed become
          prevalence of Malaysians, will result in transmission
          COVID-19 infections among family members, especially to
          the elderly and children.
          Muslims in the country are urged to use the opportunity
          during the month of Ramadan, which will be transformed to run
          activities and religious practices such as preparing a meal break
          fasting and praying with each family at home
          respectively. Apart from celebrating Ramadan with


      loved ones at home, this step can help us
      maintain and protect your beloved family members, especially
      their aging parents and young children from contracting the disease
      COVID-19.

      People are also advised to practice
      safe social distance (social distancing) at least one
      (1) meter from other people and hygiene is high
      such as washing hands frequently with soap and water.
